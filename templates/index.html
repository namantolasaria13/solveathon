SOLVE-A-THON

A 24-hour Hackathon, organized by the Hostel Committee in partnership with V-NEST, is set to ignite on the morning of April 10th. Tailored to address challenges faced by hostellers within the hostel and comprehensively target campus-related issues, this event is designed to elevate the student experience. With a focus on promoting empathy and the strategic application of intelligence across various domains, we invite you to be a part of this transformative initiative.

Organizing Partners

Hostel Commitee

The Hostel Committee of VIT Chennai is always ready to cater to the needs of the students to ease their lives far from the comfort of their own homes. At any time of the day or night, at least one or two staff members are on duty to cater to emergencies in the respective hostel. The hostel committee is sponsoring the complete event. Refreshments and meals during the event will also be served by the hostel committee.

V-NEST

VIT Chennai has a vibrant eco system for innovations including Incubation we have two incubation center to support students, faculty members for converting their startup ideas into startups with faculty members from each school to coordinate the innovation and Startup activities. VIT Social Business Incubator is started in the year 2017 funded by the British Council in association with Cambridge Judge Business School UK and V-NEST started in the year 2020 supported by the institute.

Institution's Innovation Council

The Institution's Innovation Council (IIC) is an initiative by the Government of India, under the Ministry of Education, to foster innovation and entrepreneurship among educational institutions. It aims to create a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship within colleges and universities across the country. The primary objective of IIC is to encourage, inspire, and nurture young minds to think creatively and come up with innovative solutions to real-world problems.

Themes

Think, Create, Innovate!

Hostel Hurdles

Offer comprehensive solutions to improve safety and security within hostel premises, while also designing systems or apps to track and reduce food wastage in dining facilities thus promoting cost savings.

Students' Welfare

Facilitate cultural exchange and diversity appreciation among hostel residents through events, workshops, or online platforms promoting intercultural understanding.

Campus Sustainability

Develop eco-friendly hostel solutions: reduce energy, promote recycling, implement sustainability. Optimize water/electricity for cost savings, environmental health.

Students' Wellbeing

Create solutions to assist students in their career development and financial management needs, mentorship programs, skill-building workshops, budgeting apps, and peer-to-peer lending platforms.

Healthcare Solutions

Offer services or products aimed at promoting physical and mental well-being among hostel residents, such as fitness programs, mindfulness workshops, or healthy meal options.

Open Innovation

Voice your challenges and bring forth your own solutions. Engage in collaborative problem-solving to address issues directly affecting your living or academic environment.
